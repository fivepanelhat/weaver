# Ethics Review Playbook

## Purpose
- Provide a reproducible, auditable process for performing the Responsible Innovation Framework's annual ethics review and ad-hoc assessments.

## Scope
- Applies to product features, research programs, data-sharing agreements, and any agentic automation that materially affects stakeholders.

## Owner
- Head of Ethics & Compliance (primary)
- Reviewer pool: Legal, Privacy, Security, Product, Community Representative(s)

## Inputs
- Project brief or proposal
- Data classification and data flow diagrams
- Risk assessment and privacy impact assessment (if applicable)

## Review Cycle
- Annual full playbook review.
- Immediate review after any incident involving ethical harms, privacy breaches, community concerns, or agentic failures.

Review Panel
1. Chair (Ethics owner)
2. Subject-matter reviewers (as needed)
3. Community/partner representative for culturally sensitive projects

Process (step-by-step)
1. Intake: submit an Ethics Assessment Form (use template below) at least 3 weeks before targeted launch.
2. Triage: Chair validates scope and assigns reviewers within 3 business days.
3. Review: reviewers evaluate against criteria (harms, privacy, fairness, sovereignty, security) and record findings in the decision log.
4. Meeting: panel meets to discuss high-risk items and decide one of: Approve, Approve with Conditions, or Reject.
5. Remediation: owner creates remediation plan with owners and deadlines; track in remediation tracker.
6. Publish: produce a one-page summary for internal stakeholders; redact sensitive details as required.

## Decision Criteria (examples)
- Likelihood and severity of harm
- Data residency and consent alignment
- Fairness and bias risk
- Reversibility and rollback options

## Key Risks & Controls
- Risk: ethics or privacy gaps in agentic automation. Control: independent review panel and documented mitigation plan.
- Risk: undefined consent or data residency rules. Control: mandatory data classification and residency checks before approval.
- Risk: delayed remediation of high-risk items. Control: tracked remediation deadlines and escalation to executive sponsor.

## Templates
- Ethics Assessment Form (include: project name, owner, summary, data types, affected groups, mitigations)
- Decision Log (date, reviewers, decision, rationale, remediation items)

Artifacts
- Assessment forms, decision logs, remediation tracker, published summary

Metrics & SLAs
- Intake to triage: 3 business days
- Triage to decision: 15 business days (target)
- Time-to-resolution for high-risk remediation: 90 days (target)

Exceptions & Appeals
- Exception requests follow the Policy Exceptions procedure; appeals go to the executive sponsor.

Review Cycle
- Annual full review of the playbook and after any incident involving ethical harms.
