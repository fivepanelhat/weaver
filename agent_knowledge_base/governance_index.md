# Governance Index — Coastal Alpine Tech

This one-page index summarizes the governance and operational documents that underpin the platform's Responsible Innovation, Taonga DAO, and Platinum Standard operating system.

## Governance Documents
- **Corporate Policy & Frameworks:** [corporate_frameworks.md](corporate_frameworks.md)
  - High-level policy summary of the Responsible Innovation Framework, Digital Taonga DAO Governance, and Platinum Standard Operating System.
  - Includes review cycles, owner roles, metrics, and NZ legislative anchors.

- **Ethics Review Playbook:** [ethics_review_playbook.md](ethics_review_playbook.md)
  - Reproducible ethics assessment process for product features, research, data-sharing agreements, and agentic automation.
  - Includes decision criteria, templates, SLAs, remediation tracking, and compliance anchors.

- **Digital Taonga DAO Charter:** [dao_charter.md](dao_charter.md)
  - Community-led governance for culturally sensitive digital assets.
  - Covers membership, proposal lifecycle, voting rules, access controls, and data handling requirements.

- **Platform Runbook (Platinum Standard):** [platform_runbook.md](platform_runbook.md)
  - Operational playbook for change control, deployment, testing, incident response, rollback, monitoring, and agent deployment controls.
  - Includes platform safety guidance for autonomous agents and tenant-aware operations.

- **Multi-tenant Database Schema:** [Context/multi_tenant_database_schema.md](Context/multi_tenant_database_schema.md)
  - Tenant isolation and semantic memory design for PostgreSQL/pgvector or MILVUS-based RAG systems.
  - Documents data flows, embedding governance, and tenant-aware retrieval controls.

## Review Cadence
- **Corporate frameworks:** annual review or on major legal, regulatory, or architectural changes.
- **Ethics playbook:** annual review and immediate review after any ethical incident, privacy breach, or community concern.
- **DAO charter:** annual review and updates driven by community governance outcomes or legislative change.
- **Platform runbook:** annual review and immediate update after major incidents, security findings, or platform architecture changes.
